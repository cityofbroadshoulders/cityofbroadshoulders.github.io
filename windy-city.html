Windy City Night

Chicago’s distinction as the Windy City doesn’t just refer to its blustery weather. From the Tribune to the Sun Times, Chicago has a long history of exceptional, though sometimes long-winded, storytelling. Genre-defining writers like Roger Ebert and Studs Terkel have called Chicago home, while the Second City continues to serve as a launching pad for some of the best and most respected storytellers like Tina Fey, Stephen Colbert, and Amy Poehler.

For the event’s final night, one of the country’s most celebrated storytelling producers will host a training session to help everyone tell better stories. Lea Thau is a Peabody Award-winning producer and director who served as the Executive & Creative Director of the immensely popular storytelling organization The Moth from 2001 to 2010. She now hosts and produces radio program Strangers, available everywhere as a podcast. Those attending the Windy City Night will have a chance to craft their own story and hear the tall tales of others in the community.
